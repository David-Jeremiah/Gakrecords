Our Curriculum

We offer all the essential subjects in the Old & New Curriculum of the Ugandan Education System. About Code High School

CODE high school is a mixed O’ and A’ level Christian secondary school established in 2016 with a vision to provide access to affordable, quality Christian education to children in Uganda and beyond. It is so far one of the fastest growing private secondary schools in Uganda with a focus on training tomorrows leaders today.

Why choose Code high School

Its More Than Just Education

At Code High School, we believe in nurturing the whole student – mind, body, and spirit. We provide a supportive and challenging environment where students can thrive academically, grow in faith, and develop into responsible, well-rounded individuals.

Unlocking Leadership Through Academic Excellence

Code High School is committed to empowering the next generation of Ugandan leaders. We provide a holistic education that prepares students to contribute to the development of our nation

Christian life

The spiritual life of our children is taken care of through highly enriched Christian Programs that include; counselling, discipleship, prayer, identification & nurturing of Spiritual gifts in every child under the guidance of Chaplaincy & an Active Scripture Union.
